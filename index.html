About Us

At Hugo Play, we specialize in creating safe, innovative, and engaging outdoor play spaces. With over 2 decades of experience in the playground equipment industry, we are committed to providing high-quality solutions that meet international safety standards.

Whether you're looking to design a community park, school playground, or residential play area, Hugo Play is your go-to partner for transforming outdoor spaces into vibrant hubs of activity and joy.
